== Future Horizons (v2.0+)

=== Planetary Cliodynamics
* [ ] **Interplanetary Complexity Models**: Extend secular cycle theory to model the dynamics of solar-system-scale social organizations and interplanetary resource dependency.
* [ ] **Great Transition Modeling**: Formalize the transition between planetary and multi-planetary social structures in the context of Turchin's complexity index.

=== Causal Historical Reconstruction (CHR)
* [ ] **Causal "What-If" Analysis**: Integration with `Causals.jl` to build structural causal models of historical events, allowing users to simulate alternative histories with mathematically grounded uncertainty.
* [ ] **Path-Dependency Analysis**: Formally quantify the "Sensitivity to Initial Conditions" of various historical trajectories.

=== Neuro-Cliodynamics & Sentiment
* [ ] **Collective Sentiment Monitoring**: Use LLMs and sentiment analysis to extract "Social Stress" and "Elite Mood" indicators from historical texts (e.g., newspapers, chronicles) for real-time instability prediction.
* [ ] **Psychohistorical Modeling**: Incorporate cognitive biases and "Human Factor" agents into DST models to account for irrationality in state breakdown.

=== Automated AI Historian
* [ ] **Seshat-LLM Integration**: Automate the extraction of structured cliodynamic variables from raw historical narratives and archaeological reports.
* [ ] **Automated Hypothesis Testing**: AI-driven search through the Seshat databank to find correlations and "Natural Experiments" that confirm or refute cliodynamic theories.
